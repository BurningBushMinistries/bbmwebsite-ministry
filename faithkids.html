Faith Kids

Faith Kids is the Children's department in our church.

Vision:

Making the Lord known throughout the generations to come.

Mission:

Teaching children the fear of the Lord and empowering them to share with boldness the Gospel of God [Proverbs 22:6]

Strategic Goals:

  • To see kids acknowledging the greatness of God and the importance of accepting Jesus Christ.
  • Recruiting and equipping children’s ministers.
  • Making children’s ministry child-friendly.
  • Ensuring financial stability in the Faith Kids Ministry.
