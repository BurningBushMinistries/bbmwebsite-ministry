About Us

VISION

Reaching the Nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Matthew 28:18-20

MISSION

Planting cell churches in every community.

Equipping the saints to do the work of the ministry through five fold ministry.

Each believer reaching one soul.

Raising leaders continuously.

"And the things you heard me say entrust these to reliable men who will also be qualified to teach others" (2Timothy 2: 2).

FOUNDERS

Apostle Nicholus N Mcoteli

  • Apostle Mcoteli was called by the Lord Jesus Christ as a successful business man and an award winning insurance broker, to fulltime ministry. He was at that time serving under the Full Gospel Church of God in Southern Africa.

  • Together with his wife Tandi Mcoteli, they founded their ministry in an informal settlement in East London in 1998. God tested them to the point where they lost all the material possessions they had acquired. But through it all their faith in God was not shaken.

  • Apostle Mcoteli became the first African preacher to preach via television in the Eastern Cape through the TBN Eastern Cape. God has gifted him in raising leaders. His apostolic ministry quickly spread throughout the nation of South Africa.

  • To date, he has ordained Bishops, Pastors and Elders who are responsible for the work in various churches spread across the country.

    He is also the provincial chairperson of The Evangelical Alliance of South Africa (TEASA), Eastern Cape. He is currently having a weekly radio program called ‘The Word of God is The Bread of Life’ with Link FM. By God’s grace, the Apostle and his wife founded two fully fledged Christian Schools, Faith Revival Christian School and Bizana Christian Academy, which start from Grade R to 12.

  • He is a Board Member of the Accelerated Education Enterprise (AEE), a body that oversees Christian Education in Africa and Scandinavian countries. He is a theologian, having obtained a Honors Degree in Theology from the University of Fort Hare. He is currently pursuing a Masters degree in Theology with the South African Theological Seminary. He is also concurrently studying towards his Masters degree in rural development with the University of Fort Hare.

  • He is a family man, blessed with 4 children. Together with his wife Tandi Mcoteli, they founded Olea-bond couples fellowship and they also serve as Marriage Counsellors. The couples ministry has reached multitude of families, even beyond their church walls. Apostle Mcoteli is a man of faith, a visionary and a pioneer. He is sold out for the Gospel of Jesus Christ and his passion is evident in his work.
